My love for games started when I had like two or three years old. I used to play games on a Super Nintendo with my father, we spent a lot of time playing together in my childhood and adolescence. We used to play the entire night until the dawn every weekend. After my parents separated, when I was five years old, I started playing alone because, as I said on the Sport's Page, I used to have a busy day. School, taekwondo training, soccer training every day. During the weekends, in the morning/afternoon, I prefered to play with my friends on the street because I was very hyperactive.
